## What you can do with Graph Explorer

Graph Explorer gives you a fully interactive environment to work with Microsoft Graph — the unified API surface for Microsoft 365, Azure AD, and related services.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Graph Explorer">
    Navigate to [Graph Explorer](https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/graph/graph-explorer) in your browser. No installation or account required to try it with sample data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in (optional but recommended)">
    Click **Sign in to Graph Explorer** to authenticate with your Microsoft account. This lets you query your real Microsoft 365 data and consent to permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first query">
    The query bar is pre-filled with `GET https://graph.microsoft.com/v1.0/me`. Click **Run query** to fetch your profile information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore more">
    Browse the **Sample queries** panel to discover endpoints for mail, calendar, files, Teams, and much more. Select any sample to load it instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Graph Explorer uses your real Microsoft 365 tenant data when you are signed in. You can also explore anonymously using Microsoft's built-in demo account, which provides read-only sample data.
</Note>
